    Contest Guidelines
  • Teams should consist of 3-4 students from the same school.
  • Teams should submit solutions only in the programming language that was chosen.
  • Students will be given 2 1/2 hours to complete the problems provided.
  • Standings will be based on the total number of problems solved.

    In the event of the teams solving the same number of problems, the total time taken will be used to determine standings.

  • Contest administration and code submission will be done thru the Coding Rooms platform.
  • Important - Each teacher/faculty advisor must bring with them a completed contest permission form from each student.
    Tentative Schedule for the day:
  • 3:30-3:45: Teams arrival and check-in
  • 3:45-4:30pm: Team debriefing (Submitting code, etc)
  • 4:30-7pm Contest
  • 7:00-7:30: Contest wrap-up and awards
